HOW IT WORKS

I want to ride, not navigate. So, I built Ridi, an app that takes all the guesswork out of it. You tell it what kind of roads you love – highways, primaries, unclassified’s, tracks, paths, residential? Gravel, sand? Smooth roads or tracks? Choose your style and it’ll whip up a perfect route tailored to your exact preferences. Or just choose one of the default rule sets. Whether it’s a quick spin through scenic roads or a heart-pounding journey through twisty paths, Ridi’s got you covered.
